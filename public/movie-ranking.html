The Best Movies According to Neel

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Shawshank Redemption

This is my favourite movie. It has such a good perspective on how a man saves his life from rotting in a jail.

Naruto

It teaches us the importance of standing up and protecting your loved ones.

The Theory of Everything
